My Step-by-Step Guide to Success

Here's my comprehensive roadmap to achieving my career dreams in digital marketing and event emceeing. Each step is carefully planned to build expertise and create opportunities.

1

Complete My New Media Communication Degree

Current - 2026
  • Excel in all coursework related to mass communication
  • Focus on digital marketing and corporate communication subjects
  • Maintain excellent grades to qualify for competitive internships
  • Participate actively in university media and marketing projects
2

Build My Digital Marketing Skills

Ongoing
  • Continue creating content on TikTok and Instagram
  • Master live selling techniques and audience engagement
  • Learn advanced social media marketing strategies
  • Study successful digital marketing campaigns
3

Gain Practical Experience Through Freelancing

Ongoing
  • Continue working as backstage crew for networking
  • Take on more media marketing freelance projects
  • Build a portfolio of successful campaigns and content
  • Document all achievements and learnings
4

Develop My Emceeing Skills

2025-2026
  • Start with university program hosting
  • Volunteer for wedding emceeing opportunities
  • Practice public speaking and stage presence
  • Create demo reels of my emceeing work
5

Secure Strategic Internships

2025-2026
  • Apply for internships at Shopee and TikTok in KL Sentral
  • Start with smaller companies using these platforms if needed
  • Focus on digital marketing and social media roles
  • Build professional relationships and mentorships
6

Build My Professional Network

Ongoing
  • Attend marketing events and seminars in KL
  • Connect with industry professionals on LinkedIn
  • Join marketing and media communities
  • Maintain relationships with university alumni
7

Create a Strong Personal Brand

2025-2027
  • Develop a professional online presence
  • Showcase both marketing and emceeing skills
  • Build a portfolio website with my best work
  • Establish myself as a dual-skilled professional
8

Launch My Dual Career

2026-2028
  • Apply for full-time marketing positions
  • Continue emceeing as a side business
  • Eventually balance both careers successfully
  • Become a role model for others with multiple passions
