My Journey to Media Marketing & Communications

My exploration led me to work as a freelance backstage crew, acting as an extra, doing makeup effects with actors and actresses, and working as a media marketer. I discovered the joy of creating content on TikTok and Instagram, doing live selling sessions, and connecting with people online.

Through this journey, I realized that marketing felt more calming and enjoyable compared to the high-stress environment of television media work. However, I still love media because it allows me to constantly meet new people and build connections.

My Career Vision

My dream career combines both of my passions: Digital Marketing and Event Emceeing. I want to work with big companies in marketing while also pursuing my love for hosting events and connecting with audiences.
